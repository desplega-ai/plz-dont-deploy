# Persona: Sarah Chen

## Name
**Sarah Chen** (she/her)

## Short Description

Sarah is a 28-year-old marketing coordinator living in Austin, Texas, earning $65,000 per year. She's been working full-time for 5 years and is finally trying to "adult properly" with her finances. Sarah has always been a spender - she loves trying new restaurants, buying cute clothes, and treating herself to lattes. Recently, she realized she has almost nothing saved and wants to change her habits to save for a house down payment.

**Key Characteristics**:
- **Tech comfort**: Comfortable with apps, not technical
- **Financial literacy**: Beginner, learning basics
- **Primary goal**: Cut spending by 20% to save $1,000/month
- **Pain points**: Doesn't know where money goes, forgets to track
- **Learning style**: Visual, needs simple explanations
- **Personality**: Optimistic, social, sometimes impulsive

**Financial Situation**:
- 1 checking account, 1 savings account (mostly empty)
- 2 credit cards (carries balance on one)
- $15,000 student loan debt
- No budget system, just "vibes"
- Subscriptions she forgot about: Netflix, Spotify, gym, HelloFresh

**Technology Usage**:
- iPhone user, loves Instagram
- Uses apps daily but doesn't understand how they work
- Tried Mint once but found it overwhelming
- Wants something "pretty and easy"
