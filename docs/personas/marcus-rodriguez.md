# Persona: Marcus Rodriguez

## Name
**Marcus Rodriguez** (he/him)

## Short Description

Marcus is a 42-year-old independent IT consultant based in Denver, Colorado, running his own consulting business. He makes around $120,000/year with variable monthly income depending on projects. Marcus is in that messy middle zone - he has business expenses mixed with personal ones, needs to track everything for taxes, but isn't quite big enough to justify hiring a bookkeeper. He's practical, efficient, and values his time highly.

**Key Characteristics**:
- **Tech comfort**: Moderately technical, comfortable with software
- **Financial literacy**: Intermediate, knows tax implications
- **Primary goal**: Clean expense separation, easy tax prep
- **Pain points**: Business/personal mixing, time-consuming organization
- **Learning style**: Practical, outcome-focused, likes efficiency
- **Personality**: Pragmatic, no-nonsense, values ROI

**Financial Situation**:
- Business checking account (pays himself from here)
- Personal checking and savings accounts
- Business credit card (but also uses for some personal)
- Personal credit card (sometimes uses for business emergencies)
- LLC business structure
- Pays quarterly estimated taxes
- Has an accountant he sends stuff to quarterly

**Technology Usage**:
- Mac user, comfortable with tech tools
- Uses Excel, Google Sheets daily
- Tried QuickBooks but found it overkill for his size
- Currently uses mix of spreadsheets and receipts in folders
- Wants something between "too simple" and "too complex"

**Business Details**:
- 1099 independent contractor
- 3-5 active clients at any time
- Deductible expenses: software, home office, travel, client meals
- Sometimes buys equipment that's partially business/personal
- Works from home + coffee shops + client offices
